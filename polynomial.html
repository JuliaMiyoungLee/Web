My favorite polynomial

I feel like you will probably be judging me and thinking that this is a really simple polynomial, but I think that I have a pretty genuine and reasonable reason for choosing it. I chose y=2^x because I think that it's one of the introductions that we get in math to the concept of infinity. I think that the concept of something being infinite is so cool, even though it's mad confusing in math at first. The idea that you can get infinitely close to 0, but never quite reach it is just really interesting and since y=2^x is probably one of the simplest ways of showing it and changing around the 2 or the c in ab^x+c doesn't really change the core essense of the form, I thought that this would be a pretty appropriate polynomial to choose. Also, as the x value gets larger, the way that it multiplies is also kind of mind blowing. I mean, conceptually it's not tooooo difficult to get, but the more I think about how fast these numbers are growing... yeah... It's pretty cool. Not to mention the kind of wonky hourglass shape that this table makes with the y values since the stylesheet is formatted so that the text is centered. Just a sweet addition.
